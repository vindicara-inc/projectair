02 · DescriptionVindicara · Project AIR
Problem

Agents act autonomously. Nobody can prove what happened.

  • Prompt injection, tool misuse, exfiltration, and rogue behavior happen at runtime, not in code review.
  • Observability stacks (LangSmith, Langfuse, Datadog) capture traces, not signed admissible evidence.
  • When something goes wrong, "look at the logs" is not a court answer.
  • EU AI Act Article 72 requires post-market monitoring evidence by deadline.
  • SOC 2, GDPR Art. 22, HIPAA all expect proof of human oversight on consequential actions.

The gap, in one sentence.

There is no industry-standard chain of custody for what an AI agent did, why, and who authorized it.

If you can't prove it cryptographically, you can't defend it legally. And agents are about to ship into regulated workflows whether the audit story is ready or not.

03 · ProblemVindicara · Project AIR
Solution

Four layers. One chain.

Each layer is a separate airsdk subpackage with its own demo, CLI, and crypto trust contract. A customer can adopt one without the others.

1

External Trust Anchor

RFC 3161 timestamps and Sigstore Rekor inclusion proofs over BLAKE3 chain roots. Verifiable from a clean machine with zero Vindicara API calls. Reference chain anchored on public Rekor at log index 1455601514.

2

Causal Reasoning

Walks any chain, infers step-to-step dependencies, and produces a narrowed evidence excerpt. Hard edges (CHAIN_LINK, LLM_PAIR, TOOL_PAIR) at confidence 1.0. Soft edges (OUTPUT_REUSE) at 0.5 to 1.0.

3

Auth0-verified Containment

Declarative policy halts sensitive actions and demands a step-up. HUMAN_APPROVAL records bind the action to the authenticated human who authorized it. Forged tokens cannot drive the agent forward.

4

AgDR Handoff Protocol (A2A)

Cross-agent chain of custody. Capability tokens carry a Parent Trace ID, Rekor counter-attestation proves Agent B validated it, public log carries hashed identifiers only. Wave 1 alpha shipped 2026-05-07; live demo against a real Auth0 tenant; Rekor anchor 1465403522.

13 · Uses of capitalVindicara · Project AIR
Risks

What could break this. Honestly.

Big-vendor parity

An incumbent could ship signed-chain features and slow us. Mitigation: depth on cryptography (Sigstore Rekor + RFC 3161 + ML-DSA roadmap), depth on causal layer, depth on cross-agent handoff. Each layer is independently dependable; the moat compounds.

Regulatory pace

If AI Act enforcement slips, demand for Article 72 evidence softens. Mitigation: the same artifact serves SOC 2, GDPR Art. 22, and HIPAA audits. Compliance pull is multi-vector.

Open-core monetization

OSS adoption may not convert to paid quickly. Mitigation: the upgrade path is a transport swap, not a rewrite. Pro-tier-only artifacts (premium reports, hosted dashboard, NIM-packaged deploy) are the carrots.

Founder concentration

One full-time builder today. Mitigation: capital lifts headcount immediately; the architecture is documented to a level a senior engineer can extend a layer in week one.
